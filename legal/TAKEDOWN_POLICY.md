# Takedown Policy

We respect intellectual property rights.

If you believe content hosted at this site infringes your copyright or violates your rights, please email: **takedown@ameti.one**

## What to include

- Your name and contact information
- The URL(s) at issue
- The basis for your request (copyright ownership or authorization)
- A clear description of what should be removed
- A statement that the information is accurate to the best of your knowledge

## Our response

We will:

- Acknowledge receipt within 2 business days
- Disable access while we review (when appropriate)
- Remove or restore content based on review results
- Document actions taken for audit purposes
