# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-04-14

## What we collect

This project's static site at forge.ameti.one does **not** collect personal data directly.

### Hosting analytics

Our hosting provider (Vercel) may collect standard web analytics (page views,
country-level geolocation, browser type). See [Vercel's Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy)
for details.

### No cookies

This site does not set first-party cookies or use tracking scripts.

## What we do NOT collect

- No personal information (name, email, etc.)
- No W3Schools user data
- No behavioral tracking or fingerprinting

## Data in this repository

The sample datasets in `data/samples/` contain only:
- Publicly known W3Schools page URLs
- Our own classification tags (category, topic, content type)
- Discovery timestamps

No page body content, user data, or private information is stored.
